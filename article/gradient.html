In order to create our unique and inimitable gradient, we need:

  • An empty block of the size we need. I took a large block for clarity
  • We add circles inside our block (I added 4, you can add as many as you want)
  • At this stage, we set the sizes of our circles (I decided to set different sizes for them)
  • We paint our circles in the colors you like
  • It remains to work on CSS styles. Let's add position: relative to our block and don't forget about the overflow of our block overflow: hidden;

    • It turned out pretty well, I already like it!

    Well, now a little magic. We add a bluer effect to our circles. I stopped at 150px - 200px values. You can experiment.
